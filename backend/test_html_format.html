Contract Format Example

This is how your downloaded contract will look:

1. Scope

1.1 Purpose

Agreement governs use of cloud services.

  • (i) Data storage
  • (ii) Processing services

2. Parties

2.1 Involved

Agreement between CloudCorp, provider, and User.

3. Services

3.1 Provision

CloudCorp provides access to its platform.

  • (i) Platform access
  • (ii) 24/7 support

4. Conditions

4.1 Usage

User agrees to comply with usage policies.

  • (i) No illegal activity
  • (ii) Access limits

5. Intellectual Property

5.1 Ownership

CloudCorp retains ownership of software.

  • (i) User data remains user's property

6. Liability

6.1 Limits

CloudCorp not liable for data loss.

  • (i) Maximum liability to fees paid

7. Termination

7.1 End Terms

Agreement ends upon breach or cancellation.

  • (i) 30-day notice required

8. Governing Law

8.1 Jurisdiction

Agreement governed by laws of California.

9. Signatures

9.1 Effective

Contract effective upon user acceptance.

  • (i) Electronic signatures valid

Features:

  • ✅ Clear numbering: 1, 1.1, 2, 2.1, etc.
  • ✅ Concise content (max 300 characters per section)
  • ✅ Numbered lists: (i), (ii), (iii)
  • ✅ Professional formatting
  • ✅ Easy to read and download
